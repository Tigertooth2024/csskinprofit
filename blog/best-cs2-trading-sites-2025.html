1. DMarket

DMarket is one of the largest and most popular CS2 trading platforms in the world. With its real-money marketplace and crypto integration, it offers an enormous selection of skins at competitive prices. The platform is known for excellent security and fast payouts.

Pros:
  • Huge inventory of skins and items
  • Cashout with crypto or fiat currencies
  • Strong reputation and security features
  • User-friendly trading interface
Cons:
  • Withdrawal fees can be high
  • Occasional delays during peak hours

2. Skinport

Skinport has become a favorite for traders who prioritize safety and easy payouts. Based in Germany, it's known for its verified sellers and straightforward interface. You can sell skins directly for real cash (no crypto needed) with relatively low fees compared to competitors.

Pros:
  • Low selling fees (12%)
  • Direct cashouts to bank accounts
  • Extremely trusted by the community
  • Clean, easy-to-use website
Cons:
  • No instant skin swaps (only buy/sell)
  • Limited payment options outside of Europe

3. CS.Money

CS.Money is one of the oldest and most recognizable names in CS2 trading. Known for its advanced trade bot system, CS.Money allows instant trades and offers a wide variety of skins, stickers, and agents. They also introduced a new P2P marketplace for cheaper prices.

Pros:
  • Instant trades with bots
  • Huge selection including rare skins

  • Clean UI with 3D skin previews
Cons:
  • Higher skin prices than some competitors
  • Past security breach concerns (improved now)

4. Tradeit.gg

Tradeit.gg is a fast-growing trading site with a huge focus on skin-to-skin trades. Unlike sites that require real money, Tradeit.gg makes swapping skins directly simple. It's great for users who want to refresh their inventory without cashing out.

Pros:

  • Fun giveaways and bonus programs
Cons:
  • Limited direct cashouts (more swap-focused)
  • Less popular skins sometimes overpriced

5. Swap.gg

Swap.gg specializes in skin swapping and direct trades. It's known for its fairness and fast bots. If you want to quickly upgrade or downgrade your inventory with minimal hassle, Swap.gg is a solid choice. They also offer Rust and TF2 items alongside CS2.

Pros:
  • Fast and secure trades
  • Good pricing for both selling and buying
  • Clear and simple layout
  • Bonus programs for frequent traders
Cons:
  • Smaller skin selection than DMarket or CS.Money
  • Fees can add up for big trades
